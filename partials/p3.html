Appetite is a mobile application that provides coupons to students for cheaper breafast. This project is initiated from the theme of "Survive from College". My group identified that a major problem that college students have is skipping breakfast. After brainstorming and ideation, we created a prtotype and a design specification. Within the group of 3 members, my main tasks were graphic design,wireframes and user testing. The Process Book includes details about problem idenfication, brainstorming, and prototyping.

See Specification Visit Process Book

When we identified the problem, each member came up with a wireframe for possible solution.
My idea was a coupon alarm app which provides breakfast deals only for limited morning time.

After individual testing and discussion, my group agreed on choosing my idea to develop as our solution.
We conducted heauristic evaluation on our low-fidelity mockup.

Conducting more user tests and revising mockup, we created the final prototype in the end.
