Mouda is a fintech startup in Korea which provides peer-to-peer lending platform. For 3 months, I worked as web design intern at Mouda. My main task was to design a responsive web site from scratch.

During my internship, I spent most of my time designing the home page.
At first, I designed the home page with pictures and not much texts so that
the focal points were the images.

However, after the closed beta testing, my team and I realized that
some users, who are not familiar with
navigating websites, needed a guide on how to navigate our home page.

So, I created tutorial videos and added them into our home page.
The users were now able to watch tutorials for registration, investing process and lending process
We received feedback from users saying that the tutorials were helpful.
