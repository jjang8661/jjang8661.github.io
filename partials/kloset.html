Kloset is an Android app help parents doneate outgrown child clothes and get second-handed clothes that fit their children. For this project, we conducted surveys and interview to meet the user requirement. Based on the needs, we designed a user-centered app.

Explore Prototype

After the login page, you can browse the list of clothes.
However they need Koins to request a box of clothes.

One way to get Koins is to post a box of outgrown child clothes.

With the Koins, you can request a box of clothes that would fit your child.

You can send messages to the poster of the box
and see the status of your request.

For the posters, they can manage the received requests.
